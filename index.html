Hunt
( Survival FPS )

Hunt is a videogame that was developed to be play on a phone or tablet. In this game, you are a retired hunter that went back to the mountains where he and his dad used to go hunt. However, this time it is different, the birds are the ones that are hunting you. In order to survive, you have to kill as many as possible before you get hunted. This game is a first-person shooter, the controls are simple, you move with the left joystick, the camera moves with the right joystick, and you shoot with the button labeled shoot.

Mighty Slime
( Infinite Runner )

Mighty Slime is an infinite runner that can be play on a phone, tablet or on webGL. In this game, you are a slime that only has two purposes, survive the incoming bullets, and collect as many points as possible. There is only one thing that you can do, and that is jumping whenever you, either click on the screen or press the touch of your device.

Nexus Space
( AI Based Game )

Nexus space is a school project that works entirely using AI. The goal of this “game” is to defeat the enemy by collecting materials, get rid of their defenses and destroy their planet. It is AI based which means that you, as a player, don´t control anything except for the spaceships you want to launch into battle. The enemy has sort of a behavior tree to act according to what´s currently happening on screen. Here you can only count on your analytical skills to win this space battle.
